The ETB AAV LayerZero Framework

This patent-pending framework offers the only known proactive defense.

How It Works

  1. Authenticate: Verifies the structural integrity of the packet.
  2. Authorize: Inspects the byte stream for malicious signatures (e.g., 46D1).
  3. Validate: Drops the packet before it can reach the vulnerable system.
