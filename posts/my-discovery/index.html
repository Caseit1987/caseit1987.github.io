The Discovery

I discovered a systemic architectural backdoor in firmware used across multiple critical infrastructure vendors.

The backdoor allows remote, zero-click authentication bypass and includes an explicit unlock command, proving intentional design.

I validated it against a live U.S. government IP, successfully triggering the backdoor and receiving a response.

I reported it to CISA, who assigned case VU#302619 and escalated it to emergency priority.

I built a patent‑pending defensive framework that blocks this entire class of vulnerability at LayerZero in under 0.15ms.

I did this from a non‑rooted Android phone.
